About Smash & Bite

Founded with a passion for creating the perfect burger experience, Smash & Bite brings you high-quality smashed burgers made with locally-sourced, fresh ingredients.

Our signature "smash" technique creates a crispy, caramelized crust while keeping the center juicy and flavorful. Every burger is made to order, ensuring the freshest experience possible.
